THE TATER TOT CHALLENGE

62 TOTS. 5 MINUTES.

Welcome, welcome, WELCOME to the first annual Tater Tot Challenge. This alllll began on one gorgeous summer day. I was sitting on the beach with my friend, who was working as an umbrella girl at the Beautiful Bethany Beach. A man came up to the stand to us and so innocently inquired, "Joey Chestnut ate 62 hot dogs in 10 minutes this summer. Do you ladies think you could eat 62 tater tots in 10 minutes." We looked at each other, and all we could do was laugh. He had no idea who he was talking to, or what he had just created. We said, "Sir, we could do it in five." And that day, The Tater Tot Challenge was born.

RULES

  1. Sauce allowed
  2. BYO TOTS! 62. No more, no less
  3. Have lots of fun
  4. Attire: Meatball

COMPETITION

  • Everyone that enters competes to eat 62 tater tots in 5 minutes
  • The first person to finish all of their tots wins
  • Everyone else is in it for the love of the game
  • Someone will count your tots to verify you have the right amount
Are you in?
